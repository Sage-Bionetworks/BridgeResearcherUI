A survey can have many versions, each identified by the time it was created.

To keep track of versions, we recommend you give each version a unique name, e.g. "Smoking questions added". (Participants do not see this.)

Each version can be edited independently, as many times as desired. Once you publish it, however, the survey can no longer be modified.

You can only schedule published surveys. It is common to create a schedule that returns the most recently published version of a survey, so participants automatically receive updates to the survey.

A participant's survey answers are recorded along with the specific version of the study the participant received.
