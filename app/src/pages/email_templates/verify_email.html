Email Templates

Verify Email Reset Password Email Sign In

This is the email sent to users when they sign up for your study.

It must contain the value ${url}, which is a variable that will be replaced with the address users must click to verify their email addresses. You cannot link the ${url} variable. Most web email clients will link it automatically.

There are other values available:

${studyName}
the name you entered for your study
${sponsorName}
the name you entered for the group sponsoring the study
${supportEmail}
your support email address
