Email Templates

Verify an email address as part of sign up
Verify Email Address Account Exists

This is the email sent to users when they sign up for your study with an email address, so we can verify the address.

It must contain the value ${url}, which is a variable that will be replaced with the address users must click to verify their email addresses. Most web email clients will link these URLs automatically, but you can also create a link.

There are other values available for use in your template:

${studyName}
the name you entered for your study
${studyShortName}
the short name you entered for your study
${sponsorName}
the name you entered for the group sponsoring the study
${supportEmail}
your support email address
${expirationPeriod}
the period before authentication links in the email will expire (e.g. "2 hours")
