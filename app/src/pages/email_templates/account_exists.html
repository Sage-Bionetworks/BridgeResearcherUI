Email Templates

If a user tries to sign up twice for the same study, we will send this email. It notes the account already exists, and provides a link to reset the password or sign in via email (depending on your study's design).

It must contain the value ${resetPasswordUrl} or ${emailSignInUrl}. Most web email clients will link these URLs automatically, but you can also create a link.

There are other values available for use in your template:

${resetPasswordUrl}
a link user can click to reset the password (older templates use ${url})
${emailSignInUrl}
a link user can click to sign in via email (only available if the study has email sign ins enabled)
${studyName}
the name you entered for your study
${studyShortName}
the short name you entered for your study
${sponsorName}
the name you entered for the group sponsoring the study
${supportEmail}
your support email address
