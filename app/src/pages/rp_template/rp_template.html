Verfiy Email Reset Password

This is the email sent to users when they ask to reset their password.

It must contain the value ${url}, which is a variable that will be replaced with the address users must click to verify their email addresses.

There are other values available:

${studyName}
the name you entered for your study
${sponsorName}
the name you entered for the group sponsoring the study
${supportEmail}
your support email address
