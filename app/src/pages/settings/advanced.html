Notifications

If email verification is enabled, email addresses must be verified by the user. This can happen one of two ways:

• through an email requesting verification of the email (which can be turned on or off by app developers where the template is defined), or

• by signing in through an email with a link that is sent to the userâ€™s email address. In the latter case, signing in via email only API needs to be enabled for the app.

If email verification is disabled, then the app must either require an external ID on sign up, it must validate the external ID against a list of IDs maintained by Bridge, or both must be enabled.
