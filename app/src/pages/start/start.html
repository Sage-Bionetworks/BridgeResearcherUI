Welcome to the Bridge Study Manager

The Bridge Study Manager enables you to configure your application’s server-side support.

Developers can

  • configure the consent documents and emails that are sent to users during registration;
  • create surveys to be taken by study participants;
  • schedule or re-schedule tasks and surveys that are presented to users (through a list of scheduled activities);
  • define schemas for data that is uploaded to the server;
  • adjust the server’s behavior to respond differently to new and released versions of your app.

Support staff can

  • edit users, including verifying email addresses, disabling accounts, and withdrawing users from your study;
  • resend key email or documents, such as a request to reset a password or a consent document.

Researchers can

  • view all the accounts created for the study, and the consent status of every user.
