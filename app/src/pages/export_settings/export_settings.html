Research data is exported to Synapse, a portal for researchers to manage data, collaborate with one another, and perform data analysis.

Links lead to projects in Synapse where this study's data is archived (Synapse credentials are separate from Bridge credentials so you will need a Synapse account to access).

You can also trigger manual exports, change the frequency of your study's production export schedule, and change how the reporting of errors are handled.
