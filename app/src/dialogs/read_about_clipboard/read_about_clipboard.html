Cross-Study Clipboard

Clipboard items stay in the clipboard when you switch between studies. It allows you to copy items between two studies. When you add an item, any dependencies will be copied into the clipboard as well.

To maintain these dependencies, you are only allowed to copy everything in the clipboard to a target study you sign in to, or to clear the clipboard and start over.

We'll do the right thing for this to work. For example, if you copy a schedule that references a survey, we'll copy over the survey and we'll published it too, so the survey will work in the new study.

Copying within a study will also work: it will make copies of all the dependencies, or a "deep" copy rather than the "shallow" copy available through the Copy command.
